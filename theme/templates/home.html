Lexy's Labyrinth

  • 💰 free
  • 🕹️ web (mobile OK)
  • 🎲 sokoban-like
  • ⏰ nearly endless

Free reimplementation of Chip's Challenge and its lesser-known sequel.

Tons of built-in community levels; nearly unlimited rewind and undo; a whole level editor; and support for loading just about anything. Plus some more obscure extras.

  • source code

Star Anise Chronicles 1½: Oh No Wheres Twig??

  • 💰 free
  • 🕹️ pico-8 / web (mobile OK)
  • 🎲 puzzle-platformer
  • ⏰ half an hour

Star Anise takes a short but heartfelt romp across the surface of the imaginary moon, in search of his super best friend Twig and also all the stuff he lost.

fox flux DELUXE

  • 💰 free demo

A work in progress, but you can play the first bit for free, and patrons get occasional new builds.
